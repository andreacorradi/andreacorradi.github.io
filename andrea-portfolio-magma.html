Museo delle Arti in Ghisa nella MAremma

Project Overview

Overview

MAGMA (Museo delle Arti in Ghisa nella MAremma) is a museum about cast iron art and manufacturing, and about the history of the town of Follonica. I designed the short guide, the exhibition catalogue, a flyer and the website.

Tools
  • Illustrator
  • Indesign
  • Photoshop
  • Processing
Tasks
  • Visual design
  • Print design
  • Editorial design
Client
Asteria Multimedia
Comune di Follonica

The main concept is to represent a molten material as a line warped pattern: the lines can build an abstract texture (as on the catalogue cover) or a shape resembling the artistic pieces.

The generated textures have been used to design the catalogue and short guide covers, the website background and many visual elements of the different communication materials.

The short guide drives the visitor through the exhibition path, the flyer promotes the new museum and the website collects many additional information and contents.
