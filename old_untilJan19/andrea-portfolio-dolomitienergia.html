A custom set of pictograms has been designed for the exhibition.
The grid is inspired by the shape of the turbine.

In the first introductory room, the network of hydroelectric plants of Trentino is presented through a big wallpaper map. Another big wallpaper and a scale model explain the advantages of the hydroelectric energy.

In the control room, the left hand side infographic displays a detailed analysis of the energy produced along six decades, showing contributions by different power and consumption sources.

Control room of the plant. The left hand side infographic displays a comparison between the wattage available from different power plants and a more detailed analysis of the energy produced along six decades, showing contributions by different power and consumption sources.
