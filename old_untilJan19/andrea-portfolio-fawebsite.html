Fondazione Agnelli website

Project Overview

Overview

Fondazione Agnelli is a research institute working on social sciences. With the TODO team we carried out a complete redesign of their website: firstly we re-arranged the structure of their archive to achieve an easier and clearer classification of the contents, then we designed a new architecture to accomodate them in a modern layout, to achieve a sleek and pleasing experience for editors and users.

View website
Project designed and developed with the TODO team

The homepage features a full screen sliding curtain inspired by the visual identity and a full-width grid layout displaying the most relevant contents. The different colors mark the 5 different content types.

The information architecture lets the editors distribute news, presentations, articles and papers in a hierarchical structure based on two main content collectors: projects and themes.

Particular care has been put in the search tool, in order to drive the user towards the desired content in the easiest way. It is possible to search by theme, by tag, by content type, full-text, and by every combination of the mentioned criteria.

The website is fully responsive and mobile-friendly. Even the search process has been optimized for mobile devices, by grouping the results and letting the user explore them progressively.
