Il futuro è

Project Overview

Overview

To mark their 50th anniversary, Fondazione Agnelli wanted to present 50 visions of the future, 50 years from now. With my collogues at TODO we developed the concept and designed the format, producing a series of short interviews with several experts, divided into clips that can be intertwined together by the users along themes and across subjects.

View website
Project designed and developed with the TODO team

After an onboarding sequence which gives the user the context, the website presents a scrolling wall featuring clusters of thumbnails grouped by tags.

The scroll behaviour has been custom developed to give a very smooth and sleek feeling. Having the clips grouped by tag allows an intuitive exploration and fosters the discovery of correlations between the speeches.

The wall can be configured in order to display people instead of tags: in this way the exploration becomes more traditional. In desktop navigation the people thumbnails feature video previews.

Clicking on a wall item brings to the video player window, which shows all the speeches related to the chosen tag or person. A full featured interface allows to switch clips navigating by person, tag or related tags.

A powerful search tool lets the user view all the persons or all the tags, and enables a full-text search on all the transcripts.

The website is fully responsive and carefully crafted to give an enjoyable experience on both desktop and mobile contexts.
